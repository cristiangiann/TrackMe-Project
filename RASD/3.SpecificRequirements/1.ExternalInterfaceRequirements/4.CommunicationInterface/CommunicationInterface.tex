\subsection{Communication Interfaces}
The system to be will only make use of the usual communication protocols (TCP/IP) to guarantee the connection between the \ic{User's Smartphones} and the \ic{Services}' backend systems.
\\ Other protocols, as Bluetooth for example, may be used by \ic{Smart wearables} to comunicate with \ic{User's Smartphone} but this is out of the system to be scope.
